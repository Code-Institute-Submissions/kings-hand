Cover-up

You have a tattoo you have grown out of, or from a time in your life you would rather forget?

At King's Hand we specialize in creative reinvention of unwanted tattoos, as they are smaller than covering it up with bigger and darker design. Another thing to consider when deciding between removal or cover-up is that covering up an unwanted tattoo is generally shorter and much cheaper than tattoo removal.

Poorly applied, faded, or light tattoos are the easiest to cover and can only be covered up using the same or darker ink. White ink may sometimes be applied to lighten the old tattoo before proceeding with the cover-up. For more information on cover ups and touch ups you can message us directly, please don’t forget to add an image of the tattoo you are planning to cover/ rework.

Laser Removal

Are you unhappy with a tattoo and want it removed?

Laser tattoo removal is effective treatment for the safe removal of tattoos with minimal pain and no lasting harm to the skin.

Laser light of a particular frequency is passed through the skin, where it can separate the tattoo ink into more modest particles. The immune system can easily eliminate these from the body. Initial consultation lasts 20 minutes and a normal treatment will take around 15 to 20 minutes. It usually takes 3 sessions to completely remove the tattoo
